Module: GH 10 - Evidence-Informed Decision Making Term Paper Topic

Topic 1.2: Mobile applications as Mental Health interventions

"For my GH 10 term paper, I am evaluating the effectiveness of digital self-help interventions. Specifically, I want to see if smartphone applications using Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) can bridge the health gap for migrants and transitioning communities who often lack access to traditional care."

1. The PICOS Framework

P Population Migrants, refugees, or individuals in international transitioning communities (e.g., "Newcomers").
I Intervention Smartphone-based self-help applications (specifically CBT-based or mood-tracking tools).
C Comparator Standard care (Treatment as Usual), Waiting list, or no digital intervention.
O Outcome Primary: Reduction in symptoms of Anxiety/Depression.
Secondary: Improved Quality of Life (QoL) and Accessibility.
S Study Type Systematic Reviews and Randomized Controlled Trials (RCTs).

2. Core Evidence (Pre-Identified)

I have identified the following core papers for appraisal:

Systematic Review (Chapter 2) "Digital Mental Health Interventions for Refugees and IDPs: A Systematic Review" (2022)
Provides a broad evaluation of multiple apps across different migrant contexts.
Primary Study / RCT (Chapter 3) "Effectiveness of a smartphone-based self-help intervention (Step-by-Step) for Syrians with psychological distress: A Randomized Controlled Trial"
A specific, high-quality RCT to be appraised using the CONSORT checklist.

No-Clueless Glossary (GH 10)

Abstract The "Movie Trailer" – a 250-word summary of the whole paper.
Endpoints The "Results" (e.g., how many people got better).
GRADE Ranking results 1 to 9. (7-9 is essential stuff patients care about).
RCT (Randomized Controlled Trial) A "Fair Test" where groups are split by coin flip. (The Gold Standard).
Systematic Review A study that summarizes all existing research on a topic.
Surrogate Outcome A "Stand-in" result (like measuring heart rate instead of asking if you feel better).
